without a separate conversion pass.

## Runtime Integration

Selected migrated decoration props are converted to GLB and registered in `res://scripts/unity_asset_catalog.gd`
so they can be randomly spawned as non-replicable map dressing at their authored scale. They are
not included in the hide-and-disguise catalog. Migrated weapon models are kept in the weapon catalog
only; the AK74 GLB is used by `res://scenes/weapons/ak47.tscn` as the hunter default weapon
placeholder. The complete migrated raw FBX library remains under `res://assets/unity_migrated/`
for future map building, weapon iteration, UI, audio, and character/scene experiments.

Tank Complete `Tutorial_Demo` has been rebuilt into three Godot-selectable maps:

- `res://scenes/level/maps/tank_demo_desert.tscn`
- `res://scenes/level/maps/tank_demo_jungle.tscn`
- `res://scenes/level/maps/tank_demo_moon.tscn`

The maps are generated by `res://scripts/tank_demo_map.gd` using converted GLB models, explicit
Godot material resources, static collision blockers, tank showcase animations, and migrated tank
audio nodes. They are available in the lobby map selector as Tank Demo Desert, Tank Demo Jungle,
and Tank Demo Moon.
